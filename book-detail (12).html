About the Book
A brutal murder. A trail of deceit. A quest for justice at any cost. After a scandal forces her to leave L.A., investigative journalist Meg Dawson returns to her home city of Seattle to face the past she left behind and rekindle her career. While learning the ropes of her new job at the Journal, a student at one of the city’s most prestigious private schools is murdered. The boy’s circumstances are strikingly similar to the disappearance of her best friend, Noah, last seen twelve years ago. But there are powerful forces greater than Meg at work trying to keep the truth hidden, and will sacrifice everything to keep her from solving the boy’s murder. Will Meg expose the killer before they strike again, or will her determination for justice spell her downfall? Fans of female sleuth mysteries with unforgettable characters will devour this page-turning whodunnit. Continue the Meg Dawson mystery series with Her Final Goodbye today!
